Omelette Recipe

A Simple and Delicious Classic

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 30 g grated cheese
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for the pan

Preparation

  • In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the milk, salt, and pepper.
  • Heat a non-stick pan with a bit of butter over medium heat.
  • Pour the eggs into the pan and cook for about 2 minutes, until they start to set.
  • Sprinkle the grated cheese over half of the omelette.
  • Fold it in half and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts.
  • Serve hot.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients as listed.
  2. Cook the omelette following the preparation steps.
  3. Serve immediately for the best results.
